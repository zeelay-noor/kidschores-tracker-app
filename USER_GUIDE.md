## 🚀 Getting Started

### First Time Setup

#### Step 1: Register Account
1. Open browser and go to `http://localhost:3000`
2. Click **"Register"** link
3. Fill in your details:
   - **Name:** Your full name
   - **Email:** Valid email address
   - **Password:** Strong password
   - **Role:** Select "Parent" or "Child"
4. Click **"Register"** button

#### Step 2: Login
1. Enter your email and password
2. Click **"Login"**
3. You'll be redirected to Dashboard

## 👨‍👩‍👧 Parent Guide

### Managing Children

#### Add a Child Profile
1. Click **"Children"** in navigation bar
2. Click **"Add Child"** button
3. Enter child's information:
   - Name
   - Email (unique)
   - Password
4. Click **"Add Child"**

#### Delete a Child Profile
1. Go to **"Children"** page
2. Find the child you want to remove
3. Click **"Delete"** button
4. Confirm deletion

---

### Managing Chores

#### Create a New Chore
1. Go to **"Dashboard"**
2. Click **"Add New Chore"** button
3. Fill in chore details:
   - **Title:** e.g., "Clean Your Room"
   - **Description:** Details about the task
   - **Assigned To:** Child's User ID (found in Children page)
   - **Points:** Reward points (default: 10)
4. Click **"Create Chore"**
5. 🔔 Notification will appear!

#### Track Chore Completion
- View all chores in the list
- Check status (Pending/Completed)
- See who completed what

#### Delete a Chore
1. Find the chore in the list
2. Click **"Delete"** button
3. Chore will be removed immediately

---

### Managing Study Activities

#### Create Study Activity
1. Click **"Study"** in navigation
2. Click **"Add Study Activity"**
3. Fill in details:
   - **Title:** e.g., "Math Homework"
   - **Subject:** Math, English, Science, etc.
   - **Description:** What needs to be done
   - **Assigned To:** Child's User ID
   - **Duration:** Time in minutes
4. Click **"Create Activity"**

## 👧👦 Child Guide

### Viewing Your Tasks

#### Dashboard
1. Login with your credentials
2. See all assigned chores
3. Check study activities

#### Complete a Chore
1. Find your assigned chore
2. Click **"Mark as Completed"**
3. 🎉 Earn points!
4. 🔔 Get congratulations notification

---

### Tracking Your Progress

#### Check Your Rewards
1. Click **"Rewards"** in navigation
2. View:
   - **Total Points** earned
   - **Current Level**
   - **Badges** achieved
3. Track progress to next level

#### Level System
- Start at **Level 1**
- Earn **100 points** to level up
- Each level makes you stronger! 💪

#### Badges
Earn special badges for milestones:
- 🏅 **First 50** - Earn 50 points
- 🏅 **Century** - Reach 100 points
- More badges unlock as you progress!

---

### AI Encouragement Bot

#### Get Motivation
1. Click **"AI Bot"** in navigation
2. See your personalized progress message
3. Click buttons for motivation:
   - **"Get Chore Motivation"** 💪
   - **"Get Study Motivation"** 📚

#### Test Sentiment Analyzer
1. Scroll to **"ML Sentiment Analyzer"** section
2. Type your thoughts:
   - Example: "I finished all my homework today!"
3. Click **"Analyze Sentiment"**
4. See results:
   - Sentiment (Positive/Negative)
   - Confidence percentage
   - Encouragement message

**Try different messages:**
- ✅ Positive: "I love cleaning my room!"
- ❌ Negative: "I don't want to do chores"

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Can't Login?
- Check email and password spelling
- Ensure you registered an account
- Contact parent if you're a child

### Notifications Not Working?
1. Check browser permissions
2. Allow notifications in browser settings
3. Refresh the page

### Chore Not Appearing?
- Refresh the page
- Check if assigned to correct user
- Ask parent to verify assignment

### ML Analyzer Not Working?
- Ensure ML service is running (Port 5001)
- Check internet connection
- Try refreshing the page

### Forgot Password?
- Contact system administrator
- Currently no password reset feature
- Use registered email and password
